Thematically, “Blessing In Disguise” is a follow up to the DJ Khaled anthem “Never Surrender”. Both were produced by Khaled and both feature opening verses by Scarface about the trials and tribulations of the dope life, but snitching takes center stage:

Death to them niggas getting caught go and telling

From Z-Ro:

Every time I turn around the guilty be pointing fingers at me

And finally from Rozay:

Artificial homies, worse than the agents

In conclusion from the BO$$:

Catch a case, don’t talk, that’s raw
