Rare unreleased track from Rick Ross recorded in 2002. He raps over the beat that ended up becoming T.I.’s Just Doin' My Job" off of the album Trap Muzik. For more on the backstory, click here

Given the same track, the two rappers take different approaches. T.I. takes “doing his job” to mean dealing drugs; he defends slinging crack because he’s just trying to make a living like anyone else. Rick Ross sees “doing his job” as hustling, providing for his main girl to live a lavish life

In this moderator’s opinion, T.I.’s version is more thematically focused but Rick Ross goes harder lyrically. What do YOU think?

Kanye West produced the track, sampling Bloodstone’s “I’m Just Doing My Job” in his trademark fashion
