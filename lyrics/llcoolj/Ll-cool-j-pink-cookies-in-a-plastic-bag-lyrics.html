This song out of LL Cool J’s 5th album “Pink Cookies in a Plastic Bag” is up front a song about sex, as that’s what the title is a reference to. But if you analyze the lyrics further (Don’t worry we did that already) the song actually references a lot of MC’s that was in the game at that time or had an impact. There are 2 versions of the song, the original which is on the main album, and the remix which is the music video.

For what this song sampled: http://www.whosampled.com/LL-Cool-J/Pink-Cookies-in-a-Plastic-Bag-Getting-Crushed-by-Buildings/ And for the remix: http://www.whosampled.com/remix/38383/LL-Cool-J-Pink-Cookies-in-a-Plastic-Bag-Getting-Crushed-by-Buildings
