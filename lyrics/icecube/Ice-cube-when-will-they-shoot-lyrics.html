This track is the slammin opening track on Ice Cube’s 1992 album The Predator. The track contains a skillfully mixed collection of samples, including the following: Queen’s “We Will Rock You” (stomp-stomp CLAP), “Oh Honey” by Delegation, “Treat Her Like a Prostitute” by Slick Rick, “Take Me to the Mardi Gras” by Bob James, and Cube’s own “No Vaseline."
"When Will They Shoot?” also makes many references to the L.A. riots, which had taken place earlier that same year.
