This interlude is comprised of excerpts of discourse of Malcolm X and Louis Farrakhan. They speak of issues such as the prospect of an integrated America (hence title) at a time when segregation pervaded society.
Ice Cube converted to Islam in the early 90’s which may be the reason he used discourse from these leaders.
They are portrayed as villains due to their radical movement for black power, thus serving as an appropriate prelude to the next track “Say Hi to The Bad Guy”.
