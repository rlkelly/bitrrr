Track #15 on Ice Cube’s debut solo album Amerikkka’s Most Wanted.

Ice Cube, a self-styled sexist gangsta rapper, is met with his opposite in the form of Yo-Yo, “the brand new intelligent black lady”. Here they trade bars and debate the place of women in hip-hop and the hood. Their altercation is surprisingly sided with Yo-Yo and was definitely a groundbreaking move in the gangsta scene.
