In this bomb-ass track from the Lethal Injection album, Ice Cube tells a tale of fleeing from a police helicopter, also known as a “ghetto bird”

The beat of this song, which samples Funky Worm by Ohio Players, was used in instrumental form in an early scene in the hood classic Menace II Society

Even though it wasn’t released as a single, Ghetto Bird is one of the most well-known and popular tracks from the Lethal Injection album
