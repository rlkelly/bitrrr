Ice Cube takes the role of a person with a “Hood Mentality”. He performs and follows the stereotipical ways that are associated with gangsters. THe intro is basically telling Ice Cube’s main message behind this song: We need to educated ourselves and truly develop ourselves in order to have our dreams to turn into reality.

Notice how the hook counteracts with nearly every verse, except the end of the third verse.
