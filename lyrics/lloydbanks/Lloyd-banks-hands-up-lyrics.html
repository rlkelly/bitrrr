“Hands Up” is the second single from Lloyd Banks’s second album, Rotten Apple. The chorus is sung by fellow G-Unit rapper 50 Cent. “Hands Up” peaked at #84 on the Billboard Hot 100. The song is produced by Eminem and was released as a digital download on July 25, 2006. It is also included on the soundtrack for the video game Saints Row 2.
