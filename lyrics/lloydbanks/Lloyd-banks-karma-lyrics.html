The third single from Lloyd Banks’s debut album, The Hunger for More, shows the calmer and more romantic side of Banks, which we don’t often get to see. It’s probably the closest-to-perfect description of the girl of his dreams, while singer Avant tells us, in opposition however, that he could never imagine a solid relationship with any type of female — especially this one.
