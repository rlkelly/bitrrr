The third freestyle on Chance and Lil B’s “Based Freestyle Mixtape”; it recounts both B’s and Chance’s “First Mixtape” experiences as both of them are no stranger to dropping them.

With production from Gigglebox, the song contains a sample of Nujabes' “Spiral” — which in turn contains a sample of Paul Weiner’s “Cautari”.
