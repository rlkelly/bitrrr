One of the later songs on the White Flame mixtape. Between verses, Based God takes on the persona of his fans begging him to fuck their bitches at a party and telling him how cool, funny and nonchalant he is. The beat sample’s Daft Punk’s hit “One More Time” to make a song similar to Lil B’s “Blue Flame” era which had songs like “Paris Hilton” & “Free Lil Wayne”.
