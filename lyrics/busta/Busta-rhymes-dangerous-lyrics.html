Dangerous is the second single from Busta Rhyme’s second studio album When Disaster Strikes. The song peaked at number 9 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart and was nominated for the Grammy Award for Best Rap Solo Performance.

The chorus was inspired by the 1983 Long Island Regional Poison Control Council which was targeting the dangers of loose prescription medication. The Song also samples the Extra T’S 1982 hit song “E.T Boogie”. The Song was also featured on the NBA2K15 Soundtrack which was curated by Pharell Williams.

The official music video created for the song is quite interesting and was directed by famous director Hype Williams. The film clip draws inspiration from Lethal Weapon.
