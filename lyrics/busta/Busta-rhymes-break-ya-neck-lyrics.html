This is Busta’s first single from his 2001 album Genisis. The song was produced by Dr. Dre and Scott Stortch, while the background vocals were done by Truth Hurts. The “give it away” part of the song is interpolated from the Red Hot Chilly Peppers song “Give It Away.”

The general theme of this song goes along the lines of “if haven’t broken a bone while listening to music, you’re not doing it right.”
