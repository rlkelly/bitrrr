This song is taken from one of Busta’s best selling album The Big Bang. Almost 10 years later, it was chosen to be a part of the collaborative mixtape of Q-Tip and Bust The Abstract and the Dragon.

The beat was made by the late J Dilla. It samples this great song from Minnie Ripperton. As usual, Jay Dee found the best part from the track to use, the sample only appears for a few second at around 3:20.

On the mixtape, Busta even pays his tribute to the influential beatmaker.

This song is about the Abstract and the Dragon criticizing the new MCs for not being as real and as good as they are. Definitely, a reccurring theme for Tip. For this reason even though the two of them have been in the game for so long, they are reluctant to give place to newcomers.
