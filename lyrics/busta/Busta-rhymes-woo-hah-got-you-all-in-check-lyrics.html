Woo Hah! was Busta’s first solo hit single EVER, from album: The Coming, released in 1996. There’s also an awesome remix featuring none other than the Ol' Dirty Bastard.

In ‘96 on the U.S billboard Hot 100 Woo-Hah! placed 57th, it was also nominated Best Solo Performance at the 1996 Grammys but lost to Coolio’s Gangster Paradise.

The song’s backing track is partially sampled from Galt MacDermont’s “Space” and the most memorable lyrics “Woo-Hah! Got you all in check” is a reference to Big Bank Hanks punch line from the “8th Wonder” released by the Sugar Hill Gang in 1981.

Just another awesome detail — look at each individual verse separately and you’ll see that EVERY single line rhymes with any other line in that verse.

This song is truly amazing, Mr. Rhymes really lived up to his stage name in this song!

Sample from Galt MacDermot — “Space”
