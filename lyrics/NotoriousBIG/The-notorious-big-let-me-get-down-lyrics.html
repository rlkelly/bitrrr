Perhaps ghostproduced by Kanye West. Via Vice:

We all know that Kanye produced a bunch of tracks and essentially allowed Jay Z a new lease of life. But, he first gained a great deal of experience from being mentored by D.Dot during his time at Bad Boy Records. Though the pupil soon surpassed his master by producing hits for him. These songs were often un-credited, but there is definitely a genesis of Kanye’s sound in them. Notorious B.I.G’s “Let Me Get Down” is perhaps the most audibly apparent example of a track that Yeezy was left uncredited on.
