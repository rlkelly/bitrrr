Two men scheme to commit various robberies. The voices and characters are distinct — one is skittish and hyper with a high voice, and the other is more like Biggie’s familiar persona.

Interesting Fact

Although it may not be immediately obvious, both characters are in fact voiced by the Notorious B.I.G.

This song mixes up samples of old and new music, Coldblooded by James Brown and Just to Get a Rep by Gangstarr.
