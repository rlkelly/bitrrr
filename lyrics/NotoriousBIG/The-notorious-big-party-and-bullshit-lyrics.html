Biggie’s solo debut track

The “Party and Bullshit” line is a reference to a song called “When the Revolution Comes” by The Last Poets

Biggie turned the original — which was meant to criticize people who did nothing but party and bullshit — on its head.

Per Bad Boy: The Influence of Sean “Puffy” Combs On the Music Industry, Puffy tried to get Will Smith on an new version of this song intended for Born Again. While no new version made the album, an official remix eventually came out with Biggie’s wife, Faith Evans, singing on the hook.
