From Biggie’s second and final official album, Life After Death, this is perhaps his hardest track ever. Unfortunately it’s a gem buried under the shit pile that is the worst skit of all time, which for some reason was not made a separate track

This is a diss track aimed at everybody, nobody, haters, other rappers (including Nas and certain Wu-Tang Clan members), and most importantly, YOU

The beat is produced by DJ Premier and contains a sample from “I Put a Spell on You” by Screamin' Jay Hawkins (a hard song in it’s own right), and many other small or scratched samples, a signature of any great Premo beat
