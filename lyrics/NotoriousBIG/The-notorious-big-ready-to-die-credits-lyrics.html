Here’s the track list and liner notes for the remastered version (with two bonus tracks) of The Notorious B.I.G.’s classic 1994 debut, Ready to Die. Considered by many to be the best rap album of all time, this album shook up the rap community, propelling Puff Daddy’s Bad Boy Record label to the top of the charts and solidifying B.I.G.’s place as one of the greatest rappers to ever to do it.
