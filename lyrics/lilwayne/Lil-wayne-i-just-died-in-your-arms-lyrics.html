The hook to this song is sampled from the song “(I Just) Died in Your Arms” by the English rock band Cutting Crew. In a way to differentiate it from the original, Weezy reversed the section of the title in parenthesis.

Eminem’s verse if from “Patiently Waiting” from 50 Cent’s “Get Rich or Die Trying.”
