From Tha Carter 4. We’ll let you guys decide, but many have said this is a Jay-Z Diss in response to Jay’s verse in “HAM” w/ Kanye. This samples “The Cask of Amontillado” by The Alan Parsons Project. .

According to Jadakiss, however, he has nothing to do with this.

http://twitter.com/#!/Therealkiss/status/106498022539661312

http://twitter.com/#!/Therealkiss/status/106498388421390337

Drake was also accused of dissing Jay-Z in Dreams Money Can Buy which makes sense for him to be on this song, despite Jay-Z giving him his blessing in A Star is Born

Regardless of its intent, it’s a tough joint..
