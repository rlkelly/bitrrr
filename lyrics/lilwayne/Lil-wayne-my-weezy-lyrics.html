A Young Money do-over of the high school parking lot banger “My Dougie” by Lil Wil, My Weezy appears on Dedication 3, released in November 2008. At that point, with the mixtape’s impending release many fans were hoping to hear Wayne jump on this track, and he was sure not to disappoint

As with many tracks on Dedication 3, Wayne reserves a number of verses to showcase his Young Money labelmates. And, as with many tracks on the album, this one suffers for it. Lackluster though it may be, Wayne’s verse is clearly the hottest one on the track

Overall, this track at the time of its release served to add a bit more writing to the wall, that the Golden Age of Wayne (if so it can be called), which spawned such fire on Dedication 2, Drought 3, and Carter 3, was giving way to the “new, rich(er), obsessed with feces” Wayne that today we all grudgingly endure — the Age of the Goblin
