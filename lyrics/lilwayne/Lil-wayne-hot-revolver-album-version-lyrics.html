A de-autotuned version of the song that was supposed to originally appear on Wayne’s rock album, Rebirth. The song was released on iTunes before Rebirth was released, but didn’t appear on the album.

This version for I Am Not A Human Being II was completely rerecorded (except for Dre’s hook) and a new second verse was added. View the original lyrics here.
