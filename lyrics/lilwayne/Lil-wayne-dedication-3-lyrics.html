The intro to Lil Wayne’s 3rd installment of the Dedication series with DJ Drama. Wayne, Mack Maine, Gudda Gudda & Willie The Kid rap over “The Art Of Storytelling Part 4” instrumental, which was on DJ Drama’s official Gangsta Grillz album and was a continuation of Outkast’s Art Of Storytelling series. The beat was produced by Don Cannon.
