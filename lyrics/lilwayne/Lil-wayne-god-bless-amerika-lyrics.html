One of the stand-out tracks off I Am Not a Human Being II. Wayne speaks on the harsh realities of the proverbial American Dream and the relentless elusiveness of a triumph over it.

He spells America with a ‘K’ because he’s a blood and bloods don’t say the letter ‘c’ (hint: crips).

The song bears thematic similarities to President Carter from Tha Carter IV
