This track is the third single from Lil Wayne’s sixth studio album, Tha Carter III. Main theme is rather simple; balling in the club much to the envy of all the dudes whose chicks are paying Wayne more attention than them. Complete with auto-tune.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1ohYsK5Y8Dc

Lil Wayne has claimed on his video blog {link please} that this video is based on the film Inside Man 2.
The video is directed by Gil Green.

Dallas producers Play-N-Skillz filed a lawsuit against Wayne in 2011, claiming that the Wayne owes them at least $1 million in unpaid royalties for the song.
