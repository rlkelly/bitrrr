The interlude of Tha Carter IV, with a verse from Tech N9ne and Andre 3000. Unlike most albums, the interlude here is shockingly frenetic; Tech is known for his extreme speed, such as in “Worldwide Choppers."
http://twitpic.com/6an47k/full This is the second installment to the ”Intro“ and the ”Outro“ of the album featuring Bun B, Nas, Shyne & Busta Rhymes. The reason Weezy separated Tech & Andre was to give them the full spotlight and to show how his wish came true.
