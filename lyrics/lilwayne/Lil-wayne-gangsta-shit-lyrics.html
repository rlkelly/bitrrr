Gangsta Shit is track 7 on Lil Wayne’s third studio album 500 Degreez, feat. Petey Pablo.

Released on July 23, 2002.

The Album 500 Degreez also featured some of the early Cash Money associate’s. Such as: The Big Tymers, Mannie Fresh, Birdman and less notably TQ, a west coast singer.
