“Wanksta” is 50’s first single after being discovered by Eminem; it started out as a mixtape track on No Mercy, No Fear, then added to the 8 Mile' soundtrack, and finally ended up as a bonus cut to Get Rich or Die Tryin'. Suffice to say it’s one of his more popular songs.

Although, it’s believed that the track is aimed at Ja Rule specifically, Fif said that it’s a diss toward any fake gangster. He then added that Ja fits the bill.
