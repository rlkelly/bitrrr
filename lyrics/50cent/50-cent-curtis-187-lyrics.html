‘'Curtis 187’‘ is pretty much the title track of the album. 50 Cent stated that he chose the album title because he was known as ’‘Curtis’‘ before he became famous and is channeling those days in this track. He also refers to himself as Boo Boo several times as that was the nickname he had during his younger days.
