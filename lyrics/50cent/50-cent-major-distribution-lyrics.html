‘'Major Distribution’‘ was originally released as an official single for Street King Immortal, but due to too many push-backs of the album all previous singles, including this one, was scrapped.

The song premiered on December 20, 2012 on Hot 97 and was made available for purchase on February 5, 2013. As the title of the song suggests, this of course details the many drug experiences of Fiddy, Snoopy & Jeezy.

They also shot a music video for the track, directed by Eif Rivera, which was released on January 23, 2013. The video features cameo appearances from fellow G-Unit members, Kurupt, Daz Dillinger and members of CTE World. The video also notoriously features the G-Unit members flaunting the MMG chain that was robbed from Gunplay backstage at the 2012 BET Awards.
