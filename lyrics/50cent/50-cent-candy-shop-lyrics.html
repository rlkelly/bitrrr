Easily one of his most recognisable songs, 50 Cent’s “Candy Shop” topped the Billboard Hot 100 and was nominated for a Grammy in the Best Rap Song category.

A classic club or love song all about getting that special someone back to the crib and getting hot and heavy, the lyrics are fuelled with sexual innuendo. Such innuendos including, but not limited to:
“candy shop”, “lollipop”, “hit the spot”, “wanna taste”, “magic stick”, and the most popular “I melt in your mouth girl, not in your hand”.
