After Meek Mill fired off shots at Drake for supposedly rapping a ghostwritten verse for their collaboration on Meek’s new album Dreams Worth More Than Money, “R.I.C.O.”, Drake premiered this track on the Beats 1 OVO Radio as a response. Drake slyly disses Meek while maintaining that this beef has made him an even hungrier artist.

Meek responded with two tweets shortly after the release:

https://twitter.com/MeekMill/status/625101719836434433

https://twitter.com/MeekMill/status/625107936185090048

4 days after this track, on July 29th, Drake released yet another diss named “Back To Back”

Meek finally responded on July 30th with “Wanna Know.” But he didn’t get the reaction that he was looking for though:

https://twitter.com/Whataburger/status/626959149075595264
