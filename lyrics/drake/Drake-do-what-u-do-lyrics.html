The 4th track from Drake’s first official mixtape “ Room For Improvement”. In the time of recording this song, Drake was a young rapper who is aiming for a successful career through making underground internet mixtapes.

The remix of the song was included in an other version of the mixtape, which was released in 2009, With 11 selected songs in addition to that remix.
