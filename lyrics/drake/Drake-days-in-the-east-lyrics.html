Only one day after releasing the subliminal filled rap record “Draft Day,” Drizzy is back to owl hours as he released this record in the middle of the night via his blog. He cryptically sings about a girl in his life who he holds dearly despite past issues.

The song samples a line from Rihanna’s song “Stay,” which is funny because the song is presumably about her, but who knows?
