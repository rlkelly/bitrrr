I’m going to try and make a stretch here that can make sense if you give it some real thought. The beat, flow and tempo, to me, are similar to “Slob On My Knob.”

Most of the song’s content is basically in regards to people that won’t get off him and he’s at a point in life where he’s ready to tell people “suck my dick” and move on. “People tryna drain me” is a good example that connects to “slobbing on him.”

“Slob On My Knob” also would tie in a Memphis connection that Drake has, and it would not be the first time Drake has worked directly or indirectly with Three 6 Mafia. (See Sh!t (Remix) with Juicy J for reference.)
